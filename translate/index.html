WINGS Foundation

About WINGS StiftungWINGS Stiftung also referred to as WINGS Foundation, is a not-for-profit foundation headquartered in Zug, Switzerland, exists to fulfill the WINGS Vision statement. Zug offers a neutral and internationally connected with strong fiduciary controls and a well functioning legal system.

VisionTokenization is the future, unprecedented global opportunity for the next generation of work - virtual economies that span human interests (vs geographic association)

Foundation GovernanceWINGS Stiftung is managed by a Foundation Council who delegates decisions to committees, management and advisors.

The Foundation Council is to decides how to deploy funds donated to the Foundation. In order to pursue its R&D ; objective the Board engages bought externally and internally sourced developers and organizations. Activities such as communications and advocacy rely on internal resources as well as the WINGS DAO stakeholder community.

Mutara is the statutory auditor of the WINGS Foundation.
